## <a id="creating-groups"></a>Creating Groups
</br>

1. In the administrative menu, go to _Groups_ > _List_. Or go directly to `/admin/group`.
2. Click the _Add group_ button. If more than one _Group types_ are available on the developer portal you have to select
   one before you can go further. (e.g., _Business unit_)

4. Leave the _URL alias_ text box empty.
5. Click _Create Business unit and complete your membership_ to save your changes.
6. Leave the _Generate URL alias_ check box marked.
7. Click _Save group and membership_ on the appearing page.

## <a id="managing-group-members"></a>Managing Group members
</br>

1. In the administrative menu, go to _Groups_ > _List_.
2. Find the Group you want to edit in the list and click the arrow icon next to the _Edit_ button in the _Operations_
   column at the end of the row.
3. Select _Members_ from the drop-down.

### <a id="adding-new-members"></a>Adding new members to the group

1. Click the _Add Member_ button. The _Group membership_ page opens.
2. Type the name of the user you want to add as a member in the _User_ field.
3. (optional) Assign roles to the user.
4. Click _Save_ to add the member to the group.

</br>

### <a id="editing-members"></a>Editing member settings

1. Find the member you want to edit in the list and click the arrow icon next to the _View member_ link in the
   _Operations_ column at the end of the row.
2. Select _Edit member_ from the drop-down. The edit page appears. You can add custom URL alias and assign roles to the
   member.
3. Click _Save_.

</br>

### <a id="removing-members"></a>Removing members from the group

1. Find the member you want to remove in the list and click the arrow icon next to the _Remove member_ link in the
   _Operations_ column at the end of the row.
2. Click _Delete_ to remove the member or click _Cancel_ to leave the page.
